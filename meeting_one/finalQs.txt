Questions for the client

- What time do you find it being the noisiest during the day? 
- Where do you find the noise coming from the most? ( near the pubs, smokers in the car park etc )
- where do people normally gather, what are they up to? (drinking, smoking) 
- what have you done so far to report these problems?
- what's the outcome you want from our involvement?
- do you own the property?
- do you have Internet?
- if you have the Internet, would you receive results from the device via the Internet?
- what have you done so far in regards to contacting the council?
